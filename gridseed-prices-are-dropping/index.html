Prices on Gridseeds are Dropping

25 Mar 2014

Right after this article went live, ZoomHash announced a 20 pack of Gridseed miners for $165/each here.

The announcement of the KNC Titan last week, and the inevitable spike in mining difficulty it will bring, shocked the mining world and effects are already being seen in the prices of mining hardware.

Gridseed ASIC miners in particular have seen a huge price decline of over 20% from $250 to $199 or less depending on vendor. To put it another way, the price of these miners is falling faster than the amount of coin value they can generate.

Even with the price drop the ability of these devices to break even is questionable so further price drops should be expected as vendors squeeze each others margins in a race to the bottom.
