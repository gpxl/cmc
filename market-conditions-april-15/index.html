Market Conditions : April 15

Added 15 Apr 2014 by Gerlando Piro (@gpxl)

Bitcoin values are coming back now that the April 15th PBOC deadline has passed with no declaration of a ban on BTC in China. While there's been a strong bounce in the past couple of days we need to see prices break through the $500-$525 range for a buy signal. Recent drops in the price of Gridseed miners put ROI at a more historically normal 80-90 days. A bit more upside on BTC prices and ASICs might be a deal.

After months of dread things are finally looking up!

ASIC Mining (Scrypt): Hold

Gridseed prices are at a 80 day ROI for balde miners (at $1600) and 90 days for 5 chip miners (at $130).

Bitcoin Holdings: Hold

Given the recent bounce we need to see a break through the $500-525 range before aquiring more BTC.
