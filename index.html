Scrypt Mining Update March 2014

There's no two ways about it. March absolutely sucked for miners.

The BTC/MHs/day rate dropped in half as altcoin arbitrage opportunities dried up and have yet to recover. It looks like people have quickly tired of the pump and dump schemes of the numerous altcoins that were added in recent months.

Multi-pools have been having a rough time doing much better than a few percent over straight Litecoin or Dogecoin mining, but WafflePool is still my pool of choice for simple convenience of BTC payouts.

I sold off nearly half of my GPU rigs in favor of Gridseed ASICs which took care of the heat issues I was worried about last month. As I mentioned in the latest market report GPU mining is on it's way out as ASIC scrypt miners come online.

Whether GPU or ASIC mining, the recent downturn in BTC value has made mining a lot less profitable for existing miners and almost guaranteed it to be non-profitable for new miners.

For now, I'm in a holding pattern with my current rigs as they are still profitable, but I am not investing in more hardware until there's a signal of improving conditions.
