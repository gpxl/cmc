MegaBigPower aims high.

26 Mar 2014

Ars covers the 2+ petahash, and growing, mining operation known as MegaBigPower .

The operations are fueled by thousands of mining rigs containing more than 1.4 million BitFury mining chips, while Raspberry Pis loaded with custom software direct traffic on each rig.
