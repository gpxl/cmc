Market Conditions : March 27

27 Mar 2014

Market conditions remained rough for miners throughout March and there doesn't appear to be an signs of that letting up.

GPU Mining : Sell

GPU mining is expected to continue it's decline in profitability. Non-scrypt coins might provide an extension on GPU minings usefulness. But it is advisable to sell off equipment as the opportunity arises.

ASIC Mining (Scrypt): Hold

While Gridseed miners have dropped in price recently, market conditions do not support break-even scenarios at current market prices. The introduction of much larger ASIC scrypt miners, such as the 250 MH/s KNC Titan, also threaten the ROI on Gridseed devices.

Bitcoin Holdings: Sell

The long term and short term price trends on Bitcoin continue to be negative. It is advisable for miners to sell off their mining proceeds to fiat until the trend changes.
